# Introduction to Literary Theory: A Visual Guide

This NPTEL lecture provides a comprehensive introduction to the major schools of literary theory:

- **Formalism** — Close reading and textual analysis
- **Structuralism** — Underlying systems of meaning
- **Poststructuralism** — Deconstruction and instability
- **Psychoanalytic Criticism** — The unconscious in texts
- **Feminist Criticism** — Gender and power
- **Postcolonial Criticism** — Empire and its aftermath

Ideal for undergraduates beginning their journey into literary theory.
