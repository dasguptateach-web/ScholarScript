# Urban Echoes

Neon drips on rain-slicked street,
Midnight finds the city's fleet
Of solitary souls who wander
Through the shadows, thinking yonder
Thoughts that only darkness breeds
Planting unexpected seeds.

Subway rumble, distant siren,
Every city-dweller's horizon
Tipped with light and edged with grey—
Night dissolves into the day
Through a haze of coffee steam,
Waking from a concrete dream.
