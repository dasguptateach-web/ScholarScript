## SCHOLARSCRIPT AUTO-PIPELINE ==========================

1. Double-click "ScholarScript Auto-Pipeline" shortcut on your desktop
(A terminal window opens — keep it running in background)

2. Drop any paper files into THIS folder:
- PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, TEX, ODT, RTF

3. The pipeline automatically:
✓ Extracts text and converts to Markdown
✓ Finds & attaches a related YouTube video lecture
✓ Builds the static site
✓ Deploys to your website

## 4. Done! Your paper appears at: https://dasguptateach-web.github.io/ScholarScript/papers/

Every paper page includes:
- Read aloud (TTS with Indian voices)
- Translate into 22 Indian + 9 world languages
- Embedded YouTube lecture video

Close the terminal window to stop the watcher.
